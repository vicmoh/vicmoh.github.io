The place for the things you need.

Treelea is a community founded on a vision: to create a world with more neighbours and less strangers where it’s easy to get what you need.

In the beginning we came together with a rebellious spirit to trade things out of necessity. We learned that people were inherently kind and that by leaning on each other we could make city living easier.

Since then Treelea has continued to evolve. In addition to trading goods and services you’ll now find people sharing: Local news and events, apartments and rooms for rent, job opportunities, recommendations and advice, and more!

Whether it’s a houseplant or vintage sweater, a roommate or place to call home there’s a good chance you’ll find it on Treelea as our community continues to grow.
